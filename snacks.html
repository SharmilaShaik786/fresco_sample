Veg

Veg snacks are a great way to satisfy your hunger between meals or to serve as appetizers at a party. Not only are they delicious, but theyare also healthy and packed with nutrients.One of the most popular veg snacks is hummus, which is made from samosa, rolls, nugget, French fries,wedges,aloo tikka,veg fingers,. Another tasty option is roasted vegetables.For something a bit more substantial, veggie burgers are a great choice. Made from beans, grains, and vegetables, they can be grilled or baked and served with all the traditional burger toppings. If you're looking for a snack with a bit of a kick, try spicy roasted chickpeas, which can be seasoned with chili powder, cumin, and other spices to create a flavorful and crunchy snack.Overall, there are many delicious and healthy veg snack options to choose from. Whether you're vegetarian or simply looking to incorporate more plant-based foods into your diet, veg snacks are a great way to do so while satisfying your cravings for something tasty
